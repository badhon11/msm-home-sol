What We Offer

Property Preservation & Maintenance Services

MSM Home Solution LLC provides comprehensive property preservation and maintenance for banks, lenders, investors, and realtors. Our services safeguard vacant properties, ensure compliance, and accelerate market readiness through securement, maintenance, inspections, and disaster-ready prep.

Property Securement & Boarding

We quickly secure vacant properties, board up openings, change locks, and install tamper-resistant measures to prevent unauthorized access and minimize loss.

Exterior Maintenance & Landscaping

Seasonal lawn care, debris removal, pressure washing, gutter cleaning, and hedge trimming to keep properties presentable and market-ready.

Winterization & Weatherization

Protect plumbing, HVAC, and utilities with professional winterization, moisture control, and preventive maintenance to avoid costly damages.

Storm Prep & Emergency Response

Rapid assessment, tarping, fencing, and securement after storms, plus expedited debris removal and secure storage during cleanups.

Debris Removal & Cleaning

Full-service cleanouts for foreclosed or vacant properties, including trash removal, hazardous material handling, and site sanitization.

Inspections & Monitoring

Regular property inspections with photo documentation, condition reporting, and compliance checks to protect lender interest and market readiness.
